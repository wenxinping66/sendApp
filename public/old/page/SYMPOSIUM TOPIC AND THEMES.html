ISPRS TECHNICAL COMMISSION III SYMPOSIUM ON

REMOTE SENSING

The Symposium, as one of the most important events of ISPRS Technical Commission III, is to provide an inter-disciplinary forum for scientists, researchers and practitioners in the field of “Remote Sensing”. The participants of the Symposium will present the latest developments and applications, discuss cutting-edge technologies, exchange research ideas, and promote international collaboration.
